Who are we?

We are a group of four Purdue University students studying Computer Graphics Technology. This group has been formed in the course CGT 411: Contemporary Problems in Applied Computer Graphics. Throughout the semester, we strive to create a user journey map for those who undergo experiences with the Purdue IRB (HRPP) department. Read below to find out more about the Purdue IRB (HRPP) department.

 
 

What's the Purdue IRB (HRPP)?

The Human Research Protection Program (HRPP) is charged with the responsibility of ensuring that all human subjects research conducted by faculty, staff and students under the auspices of Purdue University is conducted ethically and in a manner that promotes the protection of the rights and welfare of human subjects.
